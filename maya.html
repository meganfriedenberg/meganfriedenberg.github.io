Maya

Spring 2022 I took a 3D modeling and animation course in Maya at USC. Below is a film and models produced by me. Feel free to contact me for downloading the models!

Car desk race

For my final project for the class, we created a film by following a given storyboard. Using car stock images and my own of the 2012 Nissan Juke, I shoebox modeled it. Animation is applied to all 4 wheels. All props in the scenes were created by me over the course of the semester which includes a Nintendo switch, desk lamp, soda can, clock, and more.
